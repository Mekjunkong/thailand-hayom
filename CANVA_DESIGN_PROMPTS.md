
## 6. Cultural Tips Poster (1 page)

**Design Type:** Infographic Poster  
**Size:** A3 (297mm x 420mm) or Tabloid (11" x 17")  
**Pages:** 1 page

### Design Prompt:

Create a colorful, engaging infographic poster about Thai cultural etiquette. Use a mix of tropical colors and Thai-inspired patterns.

**Title:** "Thai Cultural Etiquette" + "נימוסים תאילנדיים"

**Sections with large icons:**

1. **🙏 The Wai Greeting**
   - Illustration of proper wai gesture
   - When to use it
   - Hebrew explanation

2. **👟 Remove Your Shoes**
   - Icon of shoes outside door
   - Where to remove (temples, homes)

3. **👑 Respect the Royal Family**
   - Crown icon
   - Never criticize or joke about royalty
   - Stand for royal anthem

4. **🛕 Temple Etiquette**
   - Dress code (covered shoulders/knees)
   - Don't point feet at Buddha
   - Speak quietly

5. **😊 Smile & Stay Calm**
   - Smiley face icon
   - "Land of Smiles"
   - Never lose temper in public

6. **👣 Feet & Head Rules**
   - Don't touch people's heads
   - Don't point feet at people
   - Feet are lowest, head is highest

7. **🍴 Eating Etiquette**
   - Use spoon and fork (not chopsticks)
   - Share dishes family-style
   - Don't waste food

8. **💰 Tipping Culture**
   - Not mandatory but appreciated
   - 20-50 baht for good service

- Use illustrations, not just text
- Bilingual throughout (English + Hebrew)
- QR code to full guide at bottom

## How to Use These Prompts in Canva:

1. **Open Canva** and select "Custom Size" or choose the appropriate template size
2. **Copy the design prompt** for the material you want to create
3. **Use Canva's AI tools:**
   - Text-to-Image for backgrounds
   - Magic Design for layout suggestions
   - Brand Kit to set your color palette
4. **Add elements:**
   - Use Canva's icon library
   - Upload Thai script fonts if needed
   - Add Hebrew text (Heebo font available in Canva)
5. **Export as PDF** (high quality, 300 DPI) for printing
