✈️ Arrival Survival Guide

מדריך הישרדות בהגעה

🛬Airport to City

Bangkok (2 Airports)

ℹ️ Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) - Main International

Airport Rail Link: 45 THB to city center (30 min)

Official Taxi: 400-500 THB + 50 THB airport fee + tolls (~70 THB)

Grab: 300-400 THB (book on arrival)

ℹ️ Don Mueang Airport (DMK) - Budget Airlines

Bus A1/A2: 30 THB to BTS/MRT stations

Official Taxi: 300-400 THB to city

💡 Pro Tip

Always insist on using the meter for taxis! If the driver refuses, find another taxi. The meter should start at 35 THB.

⚠️ Top Scams to Avoid

הונאות נפוצות להימנע מהן

🚨Common Tourist Scams

🛺 Tuk-Tuk "Temple Closed" Scam

The Scam: Tuk-tuk driver tells you the temple is closed for a special ceremony and offers to take you to a "better" temple or gem shop instead.

Reality: The temple is open. They get commission from the gem shop.

Solution: Ignore them and go to the temple anyway. Major temples are rarely closed.

💎 Gem Shop Scam

The Scam: Someone tells you about a "once-in-a-lifetime" gem sale where you can buy gems cheap and resell for profit in your country.

Reality: The gems are fake or worthless. You'll lose thousands.

Solution: Never buy expensive gems in Thailand unless you're an expert.

🚕 Taxi Meter "Broken"

The Scam: Taxi driver says meter is broken and quotes a fixed price (usually 3-5x normal fare).

Solution: Get out and find another taxi. There are thousands of taxis in Bangkok.
