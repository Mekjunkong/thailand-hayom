Fresh Fruit Yogurt Bowl — Thai-Style Healthy Snack

⏱ 5 min 🍽 Serves 2-3 📊 Easy

This refreshing fruit yogurt bowl is the perfect guilt-free snack that's become a favorite in Thai cafes and health-conscious homes. With creamy Greek yogurt, sweet honey, and fresh berries topped with crunchy granola, it's a delightful treat that takes just minutes to prepare.

healthy snackyogurt bowlfresh fruitquick breakfastthai fusion
💡 While not traditionally Thai, this healthy yogurt bowl has become popular in modern Thai cafes and health-conscious households. Thai people love incorporating fresh tropical fruits into their snacks, making this a perfect fusion of Western yogurt culture with Thai fruit appreciation.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 17.6 oz plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Fresh fruit (blueberries, raspberries, banana), as desired
  • 1/2 cup granola

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the Greek yogurt with honey and gently mix until well combined.
  2. Wash and prepare your fresh fruits - keep berries whole and slice bananas into rounds.
  3. Divide the sweetened yogurt into serving bowls or glasses.
  4. Top each serving with your choice of fresh fruits and sprinkle granola on top.
  5. Serve immediately while the granola is still crunchy.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Use thick Greek yogurt for the best creamy texture that won't make your granola soggy
  • Add the granola just before serving to maintain its crunch
  • Try using Thai tropical fruits like mango, dragon fruit, or rambutan for an authentic local twist
