Thai Fresh Fruit Skewers — Colorful Rainbow Treats for Any Occasion

⏱ 15 min 🍽 Makes 8-10 skewers 📊 Easy

These vibrant Thai-style fresh fruit skewers bring the colorful spirit of Bangkok street markets right to your table. Simple yet stunning, they transform ordinary fruits into an eye-catching treat that's perfect for parties, picnics, or healthy snacking.

thai snacksfresh fruithealthy treatsparty foodno cook
💡 Fresh fruit skewers are a beloved Thai street food and party treat, often sold by vendors who artfully arrange seasonal tropical fruits into colorful displays. In Thai culture, presenting food beautifully is just as important as taste, making these skewers both delicious and visually stunning.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 bunch green or red grapes
  • 7 oz fresh strawberries
  • 7 oz melon or watermelon, cut into cubes
  • 2 kiwi fruits, peeled and cubed
  • Additional fruits of your choice
  • Wooden skewers

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Wash all fruits thoroughly under cold running water and pat completely dry with paper towels.
  2. Thread the fruits onto wooden skewers, alternating colors to create beautiful rainbow patterns.
  3. Serve immediately while fresh and cold, or refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving for extra crispness.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Choose fruits that are ripe but still firm to prevent them from falling off the skewers
  • Cut all fruit pieces to similar sizes (about 1-inch cubes) for even presentation and easier eating
  • Soak wooden skewers in water for 10 minutes before using to prevent splintering
