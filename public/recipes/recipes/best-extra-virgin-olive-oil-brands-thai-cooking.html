The Ultimate Guide to Extra Virgin Olive Oil — 8 Premium Brands for Thai and International Cooking

⏱ 0 min 🍽 Guide for selecting olive oil 📊 Easy

Choosing the right extra virgin olive oil can transform your cooking from ordinary to extraordinary. While Thailand may be famous for coconut and palm oils, these 8 premium olive oil brands offer incredible health benefits and flavor profiles that complement both Thai and international cuisines perfectly.

olive oilhealthy cookingmediterraneanorganiccooking guide
💡 While olive oil isn't traditional in Thai cuisine, many modern Thai cooks are incorporating extra virgin olive oil for its health benefits, especially in salads like som tam or as a finishing oil for grilled meats and vegetables.

🛒 Ingredients

  • Filippo Berio Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 16.9 fl oz bottle
  • Fragata Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 16.9 fl oz bottle
  • Agrilife Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 16.9 fl oz bottle
  • Rawganiq Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 16.9 fl oz bottle
  • Clemente Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 16.9 fl oz bottle
  • Ybarra Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 16.9 fl oz bottle
  • Romulo Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 16.9 fl oz bottle
  • Naturel Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 33.8 fl oz bottle

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Choose Agrilife or Rawganiq for maximum health benefits - these organic oils are cold-pressed within 3 hours of harvest and contain the highest polyphenol levels.
  2. Select Clemente for authentic Italian flavor with Mediterranean herb notes of rosemary, orange blossom, and artichoke - perfect for drizzling over salads.
  3. Pick Romulo for mild, sweet flavor from late-harvest Arbequina olives - ideal for beginners or those who prefer gentle olive taste.
  4. Use Ybarra for its beautiful golden color and high vitamin E content - excellent for keto diets and low-heat cooking.
  5. Store all olive oils in dark glass bottles away from heat and light to preserve freshness and prevent oxidation.
  6. Taste different brands to find your preference - some prefer intense, peppery oils while others enjoy mild, fruity profiles.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Look for harvest dates within the last 12-18 months and choose oils stored in dark glass bottles to ensure maximum freshness and nutrient content
  • For Thai salads, choose milder oils like Romulo or Naturel that won't overpower the delicate balance of sweet, sour, and spicy flavors
  • Use robust oils like Agrilife or Rawganiq for marinades and dressings where you want the olive oil flavor to shine through
