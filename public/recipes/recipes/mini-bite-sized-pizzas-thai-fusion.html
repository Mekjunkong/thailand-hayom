Mini Bite-Sized Pizzas — Perfect Thai Fusion Appetizers

⏱ 27 min 🍽 Makes 20-24 mini pizzas 📊 Easy

These adorable mini pizzas are the perfect fusion of Italian comfort food and Thai-style bite-sized entertaining. Popular at Thai gatherings and parties, they're incredibly easy to make and absolutely irresistible to both kids and adults.

thai fusionappetizersmini pizzasparty foodfinger food
💡 While not traditionally Thai, these mini pizzas have become popular at Thai parties and gatherings as a fun finger food. Thai cooks love adapting international dishes into bite-sized portions perfect for sharing, making them ideal for casual entertaining.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 sheet ready-made pizza dough or crispy flatbread
  • 3 tablespoons pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings of choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cut the pizza dough into small 1.5-inch squares or use a round cookie cutter to create bite-sized pieces.
  3. Spread a small amount of pizza sauce on each piece, leaving a tiny border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over each mini pizza.
  5. Add your chosen toppings, keeping them proportional to the small size.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden, and the edges are crispy.
  7. Let cool for 2-3 minutes before serving these adorable mini bites.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Don't overload the mini pizzas with toppings — less is more for these tiny treats to cook evenly
  • If using fresh pizza dough, let it come to room temperature for easier cutting and shaping
  • Try adding a Thai twist with toppings like Thai basil, sweet chili sauce, or even a sprinkle of dried chilies
