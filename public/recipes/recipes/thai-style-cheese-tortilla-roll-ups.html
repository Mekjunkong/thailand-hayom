Thai-Style Cheese Tortilla Roll-Ups — Quick Fusion Snack

⏱ 15 min 🍽 Serves 2-3 📊 Easy

These colorful Thai-style cheese tortilla roll-ups are the perfect fusion of convenience and visual appeal. Popular in modern Thai cafes, they're incredibly easy to make and always impress guests with their beautiful spiral presentation when sliced.

thai fusionquick snackappetizercheese rollseasy recipe
💡 This modern Thai fusion snack combines Western tortillas with Thai-style presentation and serving methods. It's become popular in Thai cafes as a quick, Instagram-worthy appetizer that appeals to younger generations who love both convenience and visual appeal.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, diced (or vegetables of choice)
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon butter

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Flatten the tortillas gently with a rolling pin to make them more pliable. Spread mayonnaise evenly across each tortilla surface.
  2. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese and your choice of cooked chicken or vegetables over the mayonnaise layer.
  3. Roll each tortilla tightly, making sure the filling stays secure. Wrap each roll in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 10 minutes to set.
  4. Remove from refrigerator and slice into 1-inch thick rounds using a sharp knife. Arrange cut-side up on a serving plate to show off the colorful filling spiral.
  5. Serve immediately as an appetizer or light snack, optionally with your favorite dipping sauce.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Chilling the rolled tortillas is crucial for clean, neat slices that won't fall apart when cut
  • Use a very sharp knife and wipe it clean between cuts to maintain perfect spiral presentation
  • Try adding a thin layer of Thai sweet chili sauce instead of mayonnaise for an authentic Thai flavor twist
