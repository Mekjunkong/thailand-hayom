Homemade Chicken Nuggets — Thai-Style Crispy Perfection

⏱ 25 min 🍽 Serves 4-6 📊 Easy

These homemade chicken nuggets bring restaurant-quality crunch right to your kitchen with a simple Thai approach to seasoning and frying. Each bite delivers perfectly seasoned ground chicken wrapped in a golden, crispy panko coating that's absolutely irresistible.

thai snackschicken nuggetshomemadecrispykid-friendly
💡 While chicken nuggets aren't traditionally Thai, this homemade version reflects the Thai love for perfectly seasoned, bite-sized snacks. The technique of triple-coating ensures the signature crispy exterior that makes these irresistible.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 14 oz ground chicken breast, finely minced
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Garlic powder to taste
  • Vegetable oil for deep frying
  • Ketchup or mayonnaise for serving

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Season the ground chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste, mixing well to combine all flavors evenly.
  2. Shape the seasoned chicken mixture into bite-sized nuggets, about 1 inch in diameter, using your hands.
  3. Set up a breading station with three shallow dishes: flour in the first, beaten egg in the second, and panko breadcrumbs in the third.
  4. Coat each nugget first in flour, then dip in beaten egg, and finally roll in panko breadcrumbs, pressing gently to help coating stick.
  5. Heat oil to 350°F (175°C) in a deep pan or wok, ensuring there's enough oil to completely submerge the nuggets.
  6. Fry the nuggets in batches for 3-4 minutes, turning once, until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  7. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels before serving hot with ketchup or mayonnaise.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Don't skip the garlic powder — it adds that subtle savory depth that makes these nuggets extra flavorful
  • Make sure your oil temperature stays consistent at 350°F to achieve the perfect golden crust without overcooking
  • Press the panko coating gently but firmly onto each nugget to prevent it from falling off during frying
