Crispy Cheese French Fries — Thai-Style Loaded Fries with Homemade Cheese Sauce

⏱ 20 min 🍽 Serves 3-4 📊 Easy

Sometimes you just need comfort food that hits all the right notes, and these cheese-loaded French fries do exactly that. This Thai-inspired take on the classic snack combines crispy golden fries with a rich, creamy homemade cheese sauce that's infinitely better than anything from a packet.

thai snacksloaded friescheese saucecomfort foodeasy recipe
💡 While French fries aren't traditionally Thai, this recipe reflects how Thai cooks have embraced Western snacks and made them their own. The addition of cheese powder and the option for chili powder shows the Thai love for bold, satisfying flavors that make any dish more exciting.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1.1 lbs frozen French fries
  • 2 tablespoons cheddar cheese powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 tablespoons whole milk
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chili powder for sprinkling (optional)

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Deep fry the frozen French fries according to package instructions until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  2. In a small saucepan, melt the butter over medium-low heat. Add the cheddar cheese powder and milk, whisking constantly until you have a smooth, creamy sauce.
  3. Season the cheese sauce with salt and pepper to taste. If the sauce is too thick, add a little more milk; if too thin, cook for another minute to reduce.
  4. Arrange the hot French fries on a serving plate and generously drizzle with the warm cheese sauce. Sprinkle with chili powder if you like a little heat.
  5. Serve immediately while the fries are still hot and crispy for the best texture contrast.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Make sure your fries are completely drained and still hot when you add the cheese sauce - this prevents them from getting soggy
  • Keep the cheese sauce warm while serving by placing the saucepan in a larger pan of hot water
  • For extra indulgence, try adding a pinch of garlic powder or onion powder to your cheese sauce
